Elmer Fudd Translator

Fun app which converts from English to Fudd Speak.
Elmer J. Fudd is a fictional cartoon character and one of the most famous Looney Tunes characters created by WB Animated Universe. He speaks in an unusual way, replacing his Rs and Ls with Ws, try this to check how will he speak any English text.
